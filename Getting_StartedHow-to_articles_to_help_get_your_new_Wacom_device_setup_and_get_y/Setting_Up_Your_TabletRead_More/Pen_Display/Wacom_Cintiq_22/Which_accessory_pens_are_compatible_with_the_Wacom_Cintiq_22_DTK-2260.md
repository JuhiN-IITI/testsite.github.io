# Which accessory pens are compatible with the Wacom Cintiq 22 (DTK-2260)?

Current generation pens: 

Pro Pen 2 (KP504E)
Pro Pen Slim (KP301E)
Pro Pen 3D (KP505)



Previous generation pens:

Grip Pen (KP501E2)
Classic Pen (KP300E)
Airbrush Pen (KP400E)
Art Pen (KP701E2)
Pro Pen 1 (KP503E)
