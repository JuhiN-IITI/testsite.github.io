# Can I use Pro Pen 2 or Pro Pen with Wacom Movink?

Yes, Wacom Movink support the following pens: 
Pro Pen 2 series (Pro Pen 3D, Pro Pen slim), Pro Pen series (Grip pen, Art Pen, classic pen)
