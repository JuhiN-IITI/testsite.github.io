# What are the key features of Wacom One (DTC133)?

This pressure-sensitive pen, with 4,096 levels of pressure, provides a very natural feel when drawing digitally. Designed with Wacom technology, the battery-free and cordless pen comes with one customizable side-switch for quick access to your favorite shortcut keys.
